George V Florin Dies

Diagnostic features for the known reverse dies of the George V Florin.

Reverse C

  • The top of the crown on the harp shield points to a border bead.
  • Has a thin border bead.
  • The harp crosses are away from the border beads.
  • The word FLORIN is further away from the design.
  • The top of the F of FLORIN points to a space between border beads.
  • The lettering is large.

Reverse D

  • The top of the crown on the harp shield points to a border bead.
  • Has a thin border bead.
  • The harp crosses are away from the border beads.
  • The word FLORIN is further away from the design.
  • The top of the F of FLORIN points to a space between border beads.
  • The lettering is large.

Reverse E

  • The top of the crown on the harp shield points to a border bead.
  • Has a thin border bead.
  • The harp crosses are away from the border beads.
  • The word FLORIN is further away from the design.
  • The top of the F of FLORIN is slightly misaligned with the L.
  • The lettering is large.
