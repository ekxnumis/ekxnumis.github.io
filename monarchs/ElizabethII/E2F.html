Elizabeth II Farthings

A short series, with interesting varieties for 1953. 1956 is a little scarcer than the other dates, although most surviving specimens are in high grade.

Specifications

1953

Four varieties:

  • 1+A - only issued in plastic 9-coin 1953 sets (Scarce)
  • 1+B (Rare)
  • 2+A (Rarest)
  • 2+B - issued for currency (Very common)
