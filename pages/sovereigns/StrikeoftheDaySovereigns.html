  • Plain-edged Sovereign

2024 Strike of the Day Sovereigns

2024 D-Day 80th Anniversary SOTD

The 2024 D-Day 80th Anniversary SOTD was struck on on 6 June 2024 to honour those who participated in the Normandy Landings in 1944.


Key Information

  • Mintage: Limited Edition 1000.
  • Plain-edged Sovereign
  • Brilliant Uncirculated
  • Matt finish
  • On the reverse, below the St. George and Dragon image there is a privy mark which has a World War Two helmet above the number 80, This can be found to the left of the date, below the image of St George and the dragon.
