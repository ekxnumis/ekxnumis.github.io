British Silver Coins since 1816

Author: Peter J. Davies
Published 1982

Comprehensive listings of varieties and widely used numbering system. Includes values from 1982 useful to indicate relative scarcities.

Reference: "Davies"

The Bronze Coinage of Great Britain

Author: Michael J. Freeman
3rd edition 2006

Comprehensive listings of varieties with widely used numbering system. Includes values from 2006; Rarity scale R20-C20.

Reference: "Freeman"

English Silver Coinage from 1649

Author: P. Alan Rayner
5th edition 1992

Essential for pre-1816 silver and still very useful after that. Rarity scale R7-C3. Widely used numbering system.

Reference: "ESC"

English Copper, Tin and Bronze Coins in the British Museum 1558-1958

Author: C. Wilson Peck
2nd Edition 1970

Essential for pre-1860 copper and useful for later dates. Rarity scale PU-EXC. Widely used numbering system.

Reference: "Peck"

Identification of British 20th Century Silver Coin Varieties

Author: David J. Groom
Published 2010

More comprehensive than ESC or Davies for 1900 onwards. Includes extra varieties, detailed descriptions, and clearer photographs.

Reference: "Groom"

Identification of British 20th Century Bronze Coin Varieties

Author: David J. Groom
Published 2009

More comprehensive than Peck or Freeman for 1900 onwards. Includes extra varieties, detailed descriptions, and clearer photographs.

Reference: "Groom"

Price Guides ðŸ“ˆ

Spink's Coins of England

Annually published price guide, excellent visual reference. Covers a wider date range than other publications. Can be generous on values and ignores most varieties.

Collectors Coins GB

Published annually by Chris Perkins. Probably the most accurate of all price guides, with extensive coverage of varieties.

British Coins Market Values

Useful as a sanity check; values are lower than Spink. Basic listings with few variety references. Published annually.

Coin Yearbook

Published annually by Token Publishing. Values lower than Spink, slightly more generous than BCMV, very thin on varieties.

The Other Real Must-Have

The Standard Guide to Grading British Coins

Author: Derek Francis Allen

The definitive guide to grading coins. If every collector had a copy, online marketplaces and the hobby would benefit greatly. One of my coins is featured in it!

Reference: "SGTGB"
